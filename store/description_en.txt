ScreenSnap — Professional Screenshot, GIF Recording & Annotation Tool

Capture, record, and annotate any part of a webpage. ScreenSnap offers four capture modes, a full-featured annotation editor, and screenshot history — all running 100% locally with zero data collection.

CAPTURE MODES
- Region Screenshot (Alt+Shift+S): Drag to select any area, fine-tune with arrow keys
- Visible Area (Alt+Shift+A): Capture exactly what you see in one click
- Full Page: Auto-scroll and stitch the entire page, smart sticky header removal
- GIF Recording (Alt+Shift+G): Select a region and record up to 15s of animated GIF

ANNOTATION EDITOR
- Shapes: Rectangle, rounded rectangle, dashed rectangle, ellipse
- Arrows: Single and double-headed arrows, straight lines
- Drawing: Freehand pen and highlighter
- Text: Customizable font size, bold, italic, and color
- Callout Bubbles: Speech bubble with smooth rounded tail, auto-sized to text
- Stamps: Quick marks — checkmarks, stars, emoji, and more
- Numbers: Sequential numbered markers
- Mosaic & Blur: Redact sensitive information
- Spotlight: Dim background to highlight a focus area
- Magnifier: Zoom into fine details
- Crop: Re-frame your screenshot after capture
- Full undo/redo, keyboard shortcuts for every tool

FEATURES
- Screenshot history with thumbnails, search, and type filter (screenshot / GIF)
- Save as PNG, JPG, or WebP with adjustable quality; GIF direct download
- Copy to clipboard with one click
- Right-click context menu for quick capture
- Customizable default colors and stroke width
- Zoom controls with mouse wheel support
- Collapsible toolbar groups — clean, compact UI
- 7 languages: English, 中文简体, 中文繁體, 日本語, 한국어, Español, Português

PRIVACY
All data stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no external servers. Lightweight and open-source under MIT License.
