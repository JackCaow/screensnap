ScreenSnap — Professional Screenshot & Annotation Tool

Capture any part of a webpage with ease. ScreenSnap offers three capture modes, a powerful built-in annotation editor, and screenshot history — all running locally with zero data collection.

CAPTURE MODES
- Region Screenshot (Alt+Shift+S): Drag to select any area on the page
- Visible Area (Alt+Shift+A): Capture exactly what you see
- Full Page (Alt+Shift+F): Auto-scroll and stitch the entire page into one image, with smart sticky header removal

ANNOTATION EDITOR
- Shapes: Rectangle, dashed rectangle, rounded rectangle, ellipse
- Arrows: Single and double-headed arrows, straight lines
- Drawing: Freehand pen and highlighter
- Text & Numbers: Add labels and numbered markers
- Privacy: Mosaic and blur tools to redact sensitive content
- Full undo/redo support with keyboard shortcuts

FEATURES
- Screenshot history with thumbnails, search, and type filter
- Save as PNG, JPG, or WebP with adjustable quality
- Copy to clipboard with one click
- Right-click context menu for quick capture
- Customizable default colors and stroke width
- Zoom controls with mouse wheel support
- Bilingual: English and Chinese (Simplified)

PRIVACY
All data stays on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no external servers. Open-source under MIT License.
