# Design System Document
## Theme: Serene Grove
### Color Palette
- **Primary Color:** `#A1BC98` (A calming and earthy green, reflecting growth and nature.)
- **Neutral Color:** `#5C6059` (A deep, sophisticated charcoal that provides a strong foundation.)
- **Color Mode:** `dark` (The design is optimized for a dark interface, providing a comfortable viewing experience in low-light conditions.)
### Typography
- **Headlines:** `manrope` (Clean and modern, suitable for strong headings.)
- **Body Text:** `beVietnamPro` (Highly legible and versatile for all body content.)
- **Labels:** `beVietnamPro` (Consistent with body text for clear and readable labels.)
### Shape & Form
- **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate roundedness, offering a friendly yet structured appearance.)
### Spacing
- **Spacing:** `2` (Normal spacing, providing a balanced and comfortable layout.)
